Many components (such as Team, Gallery, or List sections) can contain multiple items. Here's how to add and publish content items within components in Sanity Studio.

<br />

<Steps>

1. **Click on the component** you want to edit.
   This opens the component editor.

2. **Find the content area** inside the component (usually labeled as "Items," "Members," "Images," or similar).

3. **Click "Add item"** at the bottom of the list.

4. **Fill in the required fields** such as text, images, links, or other settings.

5. **Save and publish.**
   The new item is saved automatically as a draft. Click **Publish** to make it visible on your website.

</Steps>
