Each page in Sanity Studio includes SEO (Search Engine Optimization) settings. These fields help search engines like Google understand your page and display it correctly in search results.

<Aside type="note">
  SEO fields are **not visible on the page itself**, but they strongly influence
  how your website appears in search results and on social media.
</Aside>

## Page title

- This is the **headline shown in search results** and in the browser tab
- It should clearly describe the page content
- Keep it short and meaningful (about 50–60 characters)

<Aside type="note" title="Example" icon="seti:smarty">
  Professional Services in Munich | Quality & Reliable Solutions
</Aside>

## Meta description

- This is the short text shown **below the page title in search results**
- It should briefly explain what visitors can expect on the page
- A good description can increase clicks from search results
- Aim for 150–160 characters

<Aside type="note" title="Example" icon="seti:smarty">
  Get professional services from experienced team members in Munich. We offer
  flexible scheduling, modern solutions, and personalized service for all your
  needs.
</Aside>

## Additional SEO information (if available)

Depending on the page, you may also see:

- **Social sharing image:** Used when the page is shared on Facebook, Twitter, or other social media
- **Indexing settings:** Controls whether search engines should index the page

## Remember

- SEO changes only take effect **after publishing** the page
- Well-maintained SEO settings help your website appear correctly in Google, look professional when shared, and reach more potential visitors
