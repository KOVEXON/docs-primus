You can add entire sections (like Hero sections, Galleries, or Testimonials) to any page.

<br />

<Steps>

1. **Open the page** where you want to add a component by navigating through the left sidebar.

2. **Scroll to the bottom** of the page content area in the editor.

3. **Click the "Add item" button** (usually marked with a + icon).

4. **Select the component type** you want to add from the list (for example: Hero section, Gallery, Testimonials, or Text block).

5. **Add content to your new component** (see step 6 below).

6. **Reorder if needed.**
   The new component appears at the bottom of the page. You can drag it to a different position by clicking and holding the drag handle (usually on the left side of the component).

7. **Publish the page** to make the new component visible on your website.

</Steps>
