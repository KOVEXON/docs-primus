Learn step by step how to create, fill out, and publish a new page in Sanity Studio.

<br />

<Aside type="tip">
  Create and publish the page first. You can always add or adjust content and
  components afterward.
</Aside>

<br />

<Steps>
1. **Open the “Pages” section in the left sidebar.**
   There you will find all existing pages of your website.

2. **Select the document type “Page” or “Legal Page.”**

3. **Click the “+” icon in the top-right corner (Create New Document).**

4. **Enter the page title.**
   Use a clear name (for example, “About Us” or “Services”) so you can easily find the page later.

5. **Set the slug (URL path).**
   The slug defines the page URL.
   Click “Generate” to create a URL automatically (for example, /about-us).
   You can adjust the slug if needed.

6. **Fill in the most important page fields.**
   Depending on the project, this may include page title, short description, header settings, or initial content sections.

7. **Add the first components.**
   Scroll to the content area and click **Add Element** to insert components such as a hero, text block, or gallery.

8. **Review your content in the editor.**
   Check texts, images, component order, and links.

9. **Click “Publish.”**
   Only then will the new page be visible on your live website.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip">
  - Use clear and concise titles.
  - Keep URLs (slugs) easy to understand.
  - Keep content organized and structured.
  - Always review the page before publishing.
</Aside>
