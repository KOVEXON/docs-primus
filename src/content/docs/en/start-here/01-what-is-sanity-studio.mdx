Sanity Studio is the central place where you manage all content on your website.

It is a simple, secure editor where you can update your website content yourself — without needing a developer.

### With Sanity Studio, you can:

- Edit and update texts across your website
- Create and manage pages
- Add, remove, or reorder sections on pages such as **Home**, **About**, or **Contact**
- Manage page content and reusable sections
- Upload, replace, and organize images
- Maintain SEO-related information like titles and descriptions

<Aside>
  All changes are saved instantly in Sanity Studio and become visible on your
  website once you **publish** them.
</Aside>
