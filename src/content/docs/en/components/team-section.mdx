### Headline

- **Type:** String (optional)
- **Purpose:** Main heading displayed above the team cards
- **Example:** "Our Team" or "Meet the Team"
- **Best Practice:** Keep it short—one sentence or maximum 2 lines

### Members

- **Type:** Array of team member objects (optional)
- **Purpose:** List of team members—each entry corresponds to one team card on the website
- **Note:** One card = one person or system (e.g., simulator)

#### Team Member Fields

**Name** (required)

- Displayed as bold heading on the card
- Example: "Max Mustermann"

**Role** (optional)

- Displayed as colored label under the name
- Keep it short and clear
- Examples: "Project Manager", "Business Advisor", "Technical Specialist"

**Bio** (optional)

- Text in the main area of the card
- Can contain multiple lines
- Longer texts may be truncated with "Show more"
- **Best Practice:** Use bullet points or short sentences, max 5-7 lines

**Photo** (optional)

- Round profile picture displayed above the card
- Should be standalone and well-lit
- **Best Practice:** Consistent style for all team members, face clearly visible

**Show Social Links** (optional)

- Toggle to display social media icons below the card
- Default: `false`
- **Best Practice:** Only enable when the person should be publicly contactable and social media is maintained

**Social Links** (optional, hidden when Show Social Links is not enabled)

- Displayed only when Show Social Links is enabled
- Icons appear below the team card
- Typical links: Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn

### Background Color

- **Type:** Reference to `backgroundColor` (optional)
- **Purpose:** Sets the background color for the entire team section
- **Note:** Does not affect individual cards

## Best Practices

**Team size:** 3-8 team members are optimal

**Consistent imagery:** Use uniform photo style for all team members

**Roles:** Keep roles short and clear

**Bios:** Keep text personal but professional

**Social links:** Only enable when profiles are actively maintained

**Photo quality:** Use high-quality, consistent photos with good lighting

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Long texts:** Avoid very long texts without paragraphs

**Inconsistent photos:** Don't use different photo formats or styles

**Empty roles:** Always fill in role fields

**Unmaintained social links:** Don't enable social links without active profiles

---

## Key Features

**Organized cards:** Team members displayed in clean, organized cards

**Photo support:** Round profile pictures for each member

**Social integration:** Optional social media links for each member

**Flexible content:** Name, role, and bio for comprehensive information

**Responsive:** Adapts to different screen sizes

## Where It's Used

The Team Section appears in these content types:

- **Pages** (`page`) – General content pages, especially about-us pages

## Technical Notes

- Photos are displayed as round images
- Social links use platform-specific icons
- Bio text may be truncated with "Show more" for longer content
- Background color references the global `backgroundColor` schema
- Cards are displayed in a responsive grid layout
