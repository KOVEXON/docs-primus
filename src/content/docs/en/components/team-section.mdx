## Overview

The **Team Section** displays all team members in organized cards. Each card shows a photo, name, role, bio, and optional social media links—perfect for introducing your team and building personal connections with visitors.

<Aside>
  **Best for:** Team introductions, staff pages, about-us sections, building
  personal connections, showcasing expertise.
</Aside>

## Fields

### Active

- **Type:** Boolean
- **Required:** No (default: **active**)
- **Purpose:** Controls whether the entire team section is displayed on the website.
- **Note:** Disable this field if the section should be prepared but not live yet.

### Headline

- **Type:** String
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** Main heading displayed above the team cards
- **Example:**  
  `Our Team` or `Meet the Team`
- **Note:** Keep it short—one sentence or maximum 2 lines

---

### Description

- **Type:** Text
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** Introductory text below the headline that briefly explains the team section.
- **Example:**  
  `Get to know the people behind our services.`
- **Note:** Keep the text compact (1–3 sentences) to explain the section without overloading the cards.

---

### Members

- **Type:** Array of team member objects
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** List of team members—each entry corresponds to one team card on the website
- **Note:** One card = one person or system (e.g., simulator). In most cases, 3–8 team members are optimal.

Each team member includes:

#### Name

- **Type:** String
- **Required:** Yes
- **Purpose:** Displayed as bold heading on the card
- **Example:**  
  `Max Mustermann`

#### Role

- **Type:** String
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** Displayed as colored label under the name
- **Example:**  
  `Project Manager`, `Business Advisor`, or `Technical Specialist`
- **Note:** Keep it short and clear

#### Bio

- **Type:** Rich text
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** Text in the main area of the card
- **Note:** Can contain multiple lines. Longer texts may be truncated with "Show more". Use bullet points or short sentences; we recommend a maximum of 5–7 lines.
- **Rich text options:** Supports formatting, images, videos, and image sliders as described in the `Rich Text` block documentation.

#### Photo

- **Type:** Image
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** Round profile picture displayed above the card
- **Note:** Should be standalone and well-lit. Consistent style for all team members, face clearly visible

#### Show Social Links

- **Type:** Boolean
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** Toggle to display social media icons below the card
- **Default Value:** `false`
- **Note:** Only enable when the person should be publicly contactable and social media is maintained

#### Social Links

- **Type:** Social links section
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** Configuration of social media links for this team member
- **Note:** Only visible when "Show Social Links" is enabled. Internally it uses the same fields as the `Social Media Links` block (section headline, description, social links list, alignment, and icon size). For details, see the `Social Media Links` documentation.

When social links are enabled, you can configure, among other things:

1. **Section headline** and **section description** (optional)
2. **Social links** (platform, URL, label, optional custom icon)
3. **Alignment** (left, center, right)
4. **Icon size** (small, medium, large)

---

### Background Color

- **Type:** Reference (`backgroundColor`)
- **Required:** No
- **Purpose:** Sets the background color for the entire section
- **Note:** Does not affect individual cards

## Usage Example

### Typical Configuration

- **Headline:** `Our Team`
- **Members:**
  1. **Name:** `Max Mustermann`
     - **Role:** `Project Manager`
     - **Bio:** Team leader, process coordinator, client relations specialist
     - **Photo:** Profile photo
     - **Show Social Links:** `true`
     - **Social Links:** Instagram, LinkedIn

## Best Practices

- **Team size:** 3-8 team members are optimal
- **Consistent imagery:** Use uniform photo style for all team members
- **Roles:** Keep roles short and clear
- **Bios:** Keep text personal but professional
- **Social links:** Only enable when profiles are actively maintained
- **Photo quality:** Use high-quality, consistent photos with good lighting

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- **Long texts:** Avoid very long texts without paragraphs
- **Inconsistent photos:** Don't use different photo formats or styles
- **Empty roles:** Always fill in role fields
- **Unmaintained social links:** Don't enable social links without active profiles

---

## Key Features

- **Organized cards:** Team members displayed in clean, organized cards
- **Photo support:** Round profile pictures for each member
- **Social integration:** Optional social media links for each member
- **Flexible content:** Name, role, and bio for comprehensive information
- **Responsive:** Adapts to different screen sizes

## Technical Notes

- The entire section can be toggled on or off via the `Active` field
- Photos are displayed as round images
- Social links use platform-specific icons and share the same options (alignment, icon size, platform selection) as the `Social Media Links` block
- Bio text may be truncated with "Show more" for longer content
- Background color references the global `backgroundColor` schema
- Cards are displayed in a responsive grid layout
