Practical tips to help you edit, review, and publish content confidently in Sanity Studio.

<br />

* **Drafts vs Published:** You can safely edit content without affecting the live website. Changes remain in draft mode until you click **Publish**.
* **Review before publishing:** Always double-check your changes before making them live.
* **Need to fix something?** You can edit and re-publish content as many times as needed.
* **Can't find what you're looking for?** Use the search function above the left sidebar to quickly locate specific content.
