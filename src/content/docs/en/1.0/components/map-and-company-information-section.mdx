## Overview

The **Map and Company Information** section combines company contact details and an interactive map into a single, comprehensive block. This all-in-one component helps visitors find your location, view your contact information, and get in touch – all in one place.

<Aside>
  **Ideal for:** Contact pages, location pages, company finder pages, and pages
  that require complete contact and location information.
</Aside>

## Fields

### Active

* **Type:** Boolean
* **Required:** No (default: **active**)
* **Purpose:** Controls whether the section is displayed on the website.

***

### Heading

* **Type:** String
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Main heading for the entire section
* **Example:**\
  `Find us` or `Contact & location`

***

### Description

* **Type:** Text
* **Required:** No
* **Rows:** 3
* **Purpose:** Short description or context for the section
* **Example:**\
  `Visit us at our location or get in touch using the contact details below.`

***

### Inner heading

* **Type:** String
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Additional heading inside the map/contact area (e.g. column heading or internal section heading)
* **Example:**\
  `How to reach us`

***

### Inner description

* **Type:** Text
* **Required:** No
* **Rows:** 3
* **Purpose:** Additional descriptive text directly in the map/contact area
* **Example:**\
  `An overview of our contact details and location.`

***

### Company information

* **Type:** Reference (`companyInformation`)
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Reference to a company information document that contains contact data such as address, phone, email, and business hours
* **Note:** It is recommended to always reference an existing company information document to ensure consistent contact details.

***

### Map

* **Type:** Reference (`mapSection`)
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Optional reference to a map document to display an interactive map with your location
* **Note:** When provided, it displays an interactive map with your business location.

***

### Background color

* **Type:** Reference (`backgroundColor`)
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Sets the background color for the entire section

***

## Usage example

### Typical configuration

* **Heading:** `Find us`
* **Description:** `Visit us at our location or contact us using the details provided.`
* **Inner heading:** `How to reach us`
* **Inner description:** `An overview of our contact details and location.`
* **Company information:** Reference to a `companyInformation` document (e.g. "Head office – city center location")
* **Map:** Reference to a `mapSection` document with coordinates and marker information (optional)
* **Background color:** Reference to a `backgroundColor` object (optional)

***

## Best practices

* **Company information:** Keep your company information document up to date – changes are reflected in every section that uses it.
* **Map integration:** Add a map for businesses with physical locations to help visitors find you easily.
* **Completeness:** Make sure all contact information in the company information document is complete and accurate.
* **Layout:** Use the heading, inner heading, and descriptions to clearly structure the section.
* **Background color:** Use sparingly – high contrast can reduce the readability of contact details.

***

## Key features

* **All-in-one solution:** Combines company information and an optional map in a single section.
* **Centralized management:** Company information is managed in one place and referenced across pages.
* **Interactive map:** Optional map for visual location display and route planning.
* **Flexible configuration:** Use the section with or without a map – company information is always the core.
* **Reusable:** Can be used on multiple pages with the same company information reference.

***

## Where it is used

* **Pages (`page`)**\
  Contact pages, location pages, and general content pages that require complete contact and location information.

***

## Technical notes

* Company information references the global `companyInformation` schema.
* Map references the global `mapSection` schema and is optional.
* Background color references the global `backgroundColor` schema.
* Inner heading and inner description provide additional structure within the section.
* The section displays company information and optionally a map in a coordinated layout.
