## Overview

The **Map and Company Information Section** combines company contact details, an interactive map, and an optional contact form into a single, comprehensive section. This all-in-one component helps visitors find your location, view your contact information, and get in touch — all in one place.

<Aside>
  **Best for:** Contact pages, location pages, business finder pages, pages
  requiring complete contact and location information.
</Aside>

***

## Fields

### Headline

* **Type:** String
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Main heading for the entire section
* **Example:**\
  `Find Us` or `Contact & Location`

***

### Description

* **Type:** Text
* **Required:** No
* **Rows:** 3
* **Purpose:** Brief description or context for the section
* **Example:**\
  `Visit us at our location or get in touch using the form below`

***

### Company Information

* **Type:** Reference (`companyInformation`)
* **Required:** Yes
* **Purpose:** Reference to a company information document containing contact details such as address, phone, email, and business hours
* **Note:** This is a required field and must reference an existing company information document

***

### Map Section

* **Type:** Reference (`mapSection`)
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Optional reference to a map section document for displaying an interactive map with your location
* **Note:** When provided, displays an interactive map showing your business location

***

### Contact Form Section

* **Type:** Reference (`contactFormSection`)
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Optional contact form to display alongside the company information and map
* **Description:** Optional contact form to display on the right side
* **Note:** When provided, displays a contact form allowing visitors to send inquiries directly from the page

***

### Background Color

* **Type:** Reference (`backgroundColor`)
* **Required:** No
* **Purpose:** Sets the background color for the entire section

***

## Usage Example

### Typical Configuration

* **Headline:** `Find Us`
* **Description:** `Visit us at our location or get in touch using the form below`
* **Company Information:** Reference to a `companyInformation` document (e.g., "Main Office - Downtown Location")
* **Map Section:** Reference to a `mapSection` document with coordinates and marker information
* **Contact Form Section:** Reference to a `contactFormSection` document (optional)
* **Background Color:** Reference to a `backgroundColor` object (optional)

***

## Best Practices

* **Company information:** Keep your company information document up to date — changes will reflect across all sections using it
* **Map integration:** Always include a map section for businesses with physical locations to help visitors find you easily
* **Contact form:** Add a contact form when you want visitors to be able to reach out directly from the page
* **Completeness:** Ensure all contact information is complete and accurate in the company information document
* **Layout:** This section works best when all three components (company info, map, and form) are used together for a complete contact experience
* **Background color:** Use sparingly — high contrast can reduce readability of contact information

***

## Key Features

* **All-in-one solution:** Combines company information, map, and contact form in a single section
* **Centralized management:** Company information is managed in one place and referenced across pages
* **Interactive map:** Optional map section for visual location display and route planning
* **Contact form integration:** Optional contact form for direct inquiries without leaving the page
* **Flexible configuration:** Use any combination of map and contact form based on your needs
* **Reusable:** Can be used on multiple pages with the same company information reference

***

## Where It's Used

* **Pages (`page`)**\
  Contact pages, location pages, and general content pages requiring complete contact and location information

***

## Technical Notes

* Company information references the global `companyInformation` schema and is required
* Map section references the global `mapSection` schema and is optional
* Contact form section references the global `contactFormSection` schema and is optional
* Background color references the global `backgroundColor` schema
* All three components (company info, map, form) can be used together or independently based on configuration
* The section displays company information, map, and contact form in a coordinated layout
