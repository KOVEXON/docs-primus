Welcome to your website documentation.

This documentation helps you manage your website content independently using the **Sanity CMS**. Here you will learn how to edit pages, update content, manage media, and configure basic SEO settings.

### What you can do with the CMS:

- Edit content and text
- Create new pages or sections
- Replace images and media
- Maintain SEO-relevant information

<Aside>
  **Important:** All changes are saved automatically as drafts in Sanity Studio,
  but they only become visible on your live website after you click **Publish**.
</Aside>
