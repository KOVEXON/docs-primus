CMS stands for **Content Management System**.

In simple terms, a CMS is a tool that lets you **manage website content easily**, similar to filling out a form.

Instead of editing complicated code, you:

* Type text into fields
* Upload images
* Click Save

Sanity Studio is the **CMS used for your website**.
