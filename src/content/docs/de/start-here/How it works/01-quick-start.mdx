This guide will show you how to manage and update the content of your website using Sanity Studio. No technical knowledge required.

<Aside>
  **Important:** All changes are saved automatically as drafts in Sanity Studio,
  but they only become visible on your live website after you click **Publish**.
</Aside>

<br />
<br />

<Steps>
1. ### Open Sanity Studio in your browser

    - Open the link to your Sanity Studio in your browser `www.example.com/admin`
    - Log in using the credentials provided to you

    {" "}

    <img src={sanityStudioUserLogin.src} alt="Sanity Studio login page" />

    After logging in, you will see the Sanity Studio dashboard with all available content types.

2.  ### Navigate the dashboard

    In the left sidebar, you will find different content sections, such as:

    - Top Header
    - Header
    - Footer
    - Company information
    - Map Section
    - Events list
    - Holiday notification
    - Promo banner
    - Blog
    - Download section
    - Pages
    - Website settings

    <img src={sanityStudioDashboard.src} alt="Sanity Studio dashboard" />

    Click on any section to view and edit its content.

</Steps>
